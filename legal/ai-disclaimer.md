# EdgeKeeper AI Disclaimer

**Usage:** This short-form disclaimer text should appear on every AI conversation surface — above or below the chat input, or as a persistent footer on conversation screens. Choose one of the variants below based on tone and space constraints.

---

## Primary Variant (Recommended)

> Mike and Ashley are AI coaching tools, not licensed financial advisors or therapists. Nothing said here is financial advice or a recommendation to trade. Your decisions are your own.

---

## Compact Variant (for tight UI spaces)

> AI coaching only — not financial advice. Your trading decisions are entirely your own.

---

## Extended Variant (for onboarding or first-session display)

> Mike and Ashley are AI-powered psychology coaches. They help you explore your trading mindset and habits — they do not analyze markets, give trade signals, or recommend financial products. Nothing in these conversations constitutes financial advice, investment advice, or professional psychological counseling. Always apply your own judgment to your trading decisions.

---

## Implementation Notes

- The Primary or Compact variant should be **persistently visible** on every conversation screen, not buried in a modal or help section.
- The Extended variant is well-suited for a one-time acknowledgment prompt shown before a user's first conversation session.
- Suggested placement: small text, muted color, below the message input field or above the conversation thread.
- Do not hide this disclaimer behind a scroll or collapse interaction on conversation surfaces.
- This disclaimer does not replace the full Terms of Service — it supplements it.
