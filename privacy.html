Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

EdgeKeeper ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what controls you have over it.

1. What We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to deliver the service:

  • Account data — email address and hashed password (stored by Supabase Auth)
  • Session data — mentor conversations, journal entries, trading rules, and notebook content you create
  • Behavioral data — discipline scores, rule violations, and Vault entries generated from your sessions
  • Account state data — Guardian Layer metrics you submit (balance, P&L, drawdown), if used
  • Usage data — message counts per month, voice session timestamps
  • Payment data — processed entirely by Paystack. We store only your subscription status, never card numbers or credentials

2. How We Use It

  • To provide your mentorship sessions and maintain continuity of memory across sessions
  • To enforce plan limits and billing
  • To trigger proactive mentor outreach (if you have enabled this in Settings)
  • To send transactional emails: billing receipts, password resets
  • To generate behavioral analytics visible only to you in your workspace

We do not sell your data. We do not use your session content to train AI models.

3. AI Processing

Your messages are sent to OpenAI's API to generate mentor responses. Only conversation content required for a response is transmitted — not your name, email, or payment information. OpenAI's data processing terms govern their handling of this data.

Voice sessions are processed by ElevenLabs. Audio is streamed in real time and is not stored by EdgeKeeper.

4. Data Storage

All data is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL) on AWS infrastructure, encrypted at rest. Row-level security policies ensure your data is accessible only to you and to server-side operations acting on your behalf.

5. Cookies

We use a single HttpOnly session cookie (ek_session) to maintain your authenticated session. It is not accessible to JavaScript and cannot be read by third-party scripts. We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you close your account, all personal data is deleted within 30 days. You may request deletion at any time by emailing privacy@edgekeeper.io.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your data; to withdraw consent; or to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. Contact privacy@edgekeeper.io to exercise these rights.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. Material changes will be communicated by email or via a workspace notice. Continued use after notification constitutes acceptance.
