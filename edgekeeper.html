You will not learn to predict markets here. You will learn to trust yourself inside them.

The Process

No forms.
No dashboards.
Just conversation.

Step 01

Private Intake

Your mentor opens with a single question. Your answer reveals everything. No surveys. No checkboxes. The profile builds itself, invisibly.

Behavioral Mapping

Through natural conversation, your mentor constructs a complete picture of your trading psychology, risk patterns, emotional triggers, and goals.

Step 03

Rules & Accountability

You codify your personal laws. Your mentor enforces them. When you deviate, they notice. The system doesn't punish. It reflects.

Step 04

Over weeks and months, your Decision Passport builds. Not a profit record but a character record. Who you were. Who you became.

Dynamic Protection

Position sizing adapts to your psychology.

On a calm, disciplined day: normal risk. After consecutive losses with your emotional state elevated, your mentor quietly reduces your maximum allowed size. Not as punishment. As protection.

Emotional Lock System

Five levels.
One objective.

The lock never touches your broker. It controls EdgeKeeper's execution layer. The conversation never stops. Only the damage does.

Level 1
Gentle notice. No restrictions. Mike mentions what he sees.
Level 2
Confirmation required. You must explain your reasoning before proceeding.
Level 3
Mandatory conversation. Mike says: "Talk to me for five minutes first."
Level 4
15-minute session lock. Trading paused. The conversation continues.
Level 5
"Today's version of you shouldn't be making financial decisions."
The Vault

The trades
you never took.

Every time your mentor stops a destructive decision, they quietly store it. Months later, during a review, they open the Vault and say: "These are the trades you never took."
